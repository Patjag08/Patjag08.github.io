Our Services

Grass-care:

We offer prestige grass care services where we offer mowing services as well as general care from replanting to fertilising. Our customers take comfort in knowing that their properties remain as green as they were when it was first constructed.

Lighting Installation:

We take pride in out lighting features, it often being the highlight to our work. We offer anything from trail lighting to flood lighting. This wide range of selection gives our customers the peace of mind while letting them still enjoy the beautiful outdoors during the night.

Fencing:

Whether you need a fence correcting or replaced completely we try our hardest to create a sturdy fence while not being an eye sore. We offer competitive pricing for our fencing and finish it in record time!

Water Features:

Water features are one of our many attractions, whether its a small pond or fountain feature. We also look out for the animals offering protective netting to help prevent your fuzzy friends falling in. These water features often coincide with the lighting feature complimenting each other perfectly.
